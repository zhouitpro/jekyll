--- layout: post title: split和preg_split的区别 tags: - PHP status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- 都可是使用正则来分割字符串 
$string = ' and 1open 2 the 3 template 4 in 5 the 6editor';
var_dump(split('[0-9]+', $string));
var_dump(split(' ', $string));  //空格来分割字符串
var_dump(preg_split('/[0-9]+/', $string));
