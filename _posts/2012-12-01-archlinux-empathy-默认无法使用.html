安装Yaourt,安装之前需要初始化key 
$sudo pacman-key --init
Yaourt的安装和使用: https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Yaourt_(%E7%AE%80%E4%BD%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87) 安装pacman-color 
$sudo yaourt pacman-color
$vim .bashrc
 alias pacman='sudo pacman-color'
 alias grep='grep -n --color=auto'
